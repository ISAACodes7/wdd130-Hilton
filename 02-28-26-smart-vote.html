The Smart vote is the Fool?

By Truman Gottem

02-28-26

The Fool Called V has entered into the running against Skrix as Voice of the Senate. Such a speaker can only fall to a man that speaks for the people, and between the two of them Mr V is the only one that seemed to allow a little bit of freedom in this rapidly changing political landscape.

In his own press conference he makes his intentions clear,
"I seek to allow the community express themselves and value partnership
"I seek to pursue commerce and grow the economy by hearing all sides of an argument before making a choice.
"If I get elected, I will host several people on my radio show."

A man of Laissez-faire economics and provider of the arts is in this humble reporters opinion a shoe in for the Position. Skrix will have to learn how to include the people he wants to speak for to get this vote.
