Justice for Orlando

By Anonymous

03-07-26

We discovered a stowaway aboard the ship, the favorite granddaughter of the matriarch. She seems to have some magic and can transform into a jaguar. After talking with her, we found the island with the temple that we've been searching for, but their was a cult of lizard people leaving on it.

On the island, we found that the dragon guy from the train arch was working with the lizard people. When Hu Man Shield saw them, he freaked out and attacked.

Rather than fighting all the lizard folk, we challenged the dragon guy to a ritual fight where the six of us and six of them got together into a snail pen and fought to the death. We won, but at least moment before his death, the dragon guy breathed a devastating fire attack that killed hu man shield. He refused to be revived.

After that, we had to deal with some lizard folk bureaucracy. We were finally let into the temple where we found what we needed to translate the seerer stones.
