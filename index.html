Welcome to the Archive!

Welcome to The Sharn Inquisitor Archive, the online repository for The Hold’s one and only newspaper. This site is meant to act as an online hub for all the articles that have been written by the newspaper guild; ideally, it could also function as an archive for the lore of The Hold itself, since there’s no place that currently keeps records of all that information. Below is a timeline of all currently published articles, from newest to oldest. Clicking on an entry should take you to that article’s page. If you have anything you’d like us to report on, you can get ahold of us here.
